The Makah

You're a guest on Makah land. A little history.

Since time immemorial

Home to the Makah people.

Sea and land woven together — permanent villages, canoe travel, and a life built on salmon, halibut, seals, and whales.

Makah history
1855

Treaty of Neah Bay.

Whaling, sealing, fishing, and village land rights were named and reserved in the treaty — rights the Makah still carry today.
